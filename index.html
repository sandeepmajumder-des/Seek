Introduction to Whatfix

Whatfix turns any web app into one that explains itself — step-by-step help, right where your team is already working.

Scene 02 21s

Whatfix guidance

A Guidance Flow walks someone through a task — creating a deal in Salesforce, filing an expense, onboarding a new hire.

You build it once by clicking through the task yourself, and Whatfix records every step. The result is a live walkthrough that anyone on that page can launch.

Flows update themselves when the app changes — so your training never goes stale.

Scene 03 14s 3s over

Styling in guidance

Every callout, popup, and tip can be styled to match your brand. Change the highlight colour, font, and corner radius from the Theme panel, then save the look as a brand kit and apply it across every flow.

Scene 04 12s

Setting up Whatfix

To install, paste the Whatfix snippet into your app's HTML, then invite your team. Anyone with a link can watch flows — admins can build new ones.
